Debian Syntax 2.4.1
===================

Removed:
  - Syntax-specific view settings for control, changelog, and copyright
    (tabs-to-spaces, trailing newline, 79-char ruler). These were opinionated
    editor defaults that don't match the convention of official Sublime Text
    syntax packages.

New:
  - Added .gitattributes to exclude non-essential files from the installed
    package.

Full changelog:
  https://github.com/barnumbirr/sublime-debian/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md
